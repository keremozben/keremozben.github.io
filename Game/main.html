KEREM ONLINE

Developed by Kerem Özben

Designed by Ertürk Emre Ay

The aim of this game is to fill all the squares starting from 1 to square count. Rules are simple;

  • You can start any square you want,
  • To advance, skip 2 squares horizontally or vertically, or skip 1 square diagonally and place next number,
  • Repeat this until you fill all the squares.
  • Be careful! As you go further, it becomes harder to find available squares, think twice before your move!
